Business-rule coverage

01Customers may be veterans, professional surfers, both, or neither.
02Customers may have multiple phone numbers and may exist before placing an order.
03Board models use fixed, company-defined component recipes; custom substitutions are excluded.
04One order belongs to one customer and may contain multiple board models and quantities.
05Quarterly analysis identifies top spenders, best sellers, and reviews that need follow-up.
